About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Sustainable Engineering and the Built Environment at Arizona State University specializing in Hydrosystems Engineering. My research explores urban hydrology at the intersection of climate science, infrastructure modeling, and resilience engineering, with an emphasis on cascading failures across interdependent systems.

Affiliated with the Hydroclimate & Infrastructure Research Lab (Prof. Giuseppe Mascaro) and the Resilient Infrastructure Laboratory (Prof. Mikhail Chester).
